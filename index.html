East Hamilton High School Class of 2016 Class Reunion

EHHS Class of 2016 - can you believe it's been 10 years? We'd like to extend a warm welcome home for our 10 year reunion! We have a weekend of fun planned during homecoming weekend this Fall!

Who: EHHS Class of 2016
When: September 4-6, 2026 (Football Homecoming Weekend)
Where: Ooltewah, TN and Chattanooga, TN
Suggested Donation: $15/person (please see additional information below)

To be transparent, there are no school or organizational funds available to cover costs for refreshments, decorations, and event supplies. To help make this reunion possible, we are asking attendees for a suggested contribution of $15 per person to help share event expenses. These contributions will go directly toward food, drinks, decorations, and other reunion supplies. Our goal is to keep this event accessible to everyone, and we don’t want financial hardship to prevent attendance. If $15 per person isn’t feasible, we simply ask that you contribute what you can ($10, $5, $0, etc.). If you’re unable to contribute financially, we also encourage bringing a shareable meal, snack, or other item to support the event. Without attendee contributions, reunion costs would otherwise be covered personally by the organizing committee. We appreciate any support in helping make this a memorable event for everyone. Weekend Events and Details

Friday, September 4, 2026 | 5:30 p.m. to kickoff

Tailgate for Homecoming Football Game

The weekend kicks off with a tailgate for the homecoming football game. The event will begin at 5:30 p.m. and continue until kickoff. We will have plenty of snacks and drinks. Parking at the school is $5 and tickets for entry to the football game are $10.

Saturday, September 5, 2026 | 4 p.m.

Happy Hour - Location to be announced

The celebration continues on Saturday with a happy hour at X in downtown Chattanooga. For this event, everyone will be responsible for their own bill.

Sunday, September 6, 2026 | 9-11 a.m.

Pastry Breakfast Send-Off

We will end the weekend with a pastry and coffee breakfast send-off in Little Debbie park.

FAQ

Do I have to attend every event?

No! Come and go as you please, we want to make these events as accessible and chill for everyone as possible.

I didn't RSVP, can I still come?

Yes! The RSVP form will be used to help us determine how much supplies to buy. Even if you didn't RSVP, we'd still love to see you!

How much are tickets?

We are asking for suggested donations of $15/person. The funds will go toward food, drinks, and supplies for the Friday evening tailgate and Sunday morning send off. Please note: we want to include as many folks as possible, so please pay just what you can ($20, $3, $0, whatever).

Payments can be made using the following methods:
Venmo: @Hannah-Dattilo
Cashapp: $HannahDattilo
Zelle: 817-404-2472

Are there any other associated costs for these events?

Yes. For the homecoming football game tailgate, parking is $5 and tickets for the game are $10. The happy hour on Saturday evening will be pay for yourself. There will be no additional cost for the Sunday morning send off.

Can I bring my kids?

Yes, all events are kid-friendly. Please note that you are responsible for them. We will not be providing any child care.
